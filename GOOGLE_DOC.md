# Wealth Management Website

A wealth management website that helps people who **don't have $1 million** manage their money.

The website should:
- Look modern and sleek to appeal to young users
- Be easy to use for everyone
- Clearly explain every tab and step
- Be accessible to people with no financial knowledge

---

## Disclaimer

**WE ARE NOT PROVIDING FINANCIAL ADVICE. THE AI COULD MAKE MISTAKES.**

---

# Core Functionalities

## Legal & Information
- Terms and Conditions page
- Disclaimer page

## Financial Tools

### Tax Simulator
- Calculate how much taxes you will pay based on:
  - Income
  - Location (province)
- Provide suggestions to reduce taxes using:
  - FHSA
  - RRSP
  - TFSA

---

### Registered Accounts Explainer

Explain in simple language:

- What is a TFSA
- What is an RRSP
- What is an FHSA
- How they work
- How money is invested inside
- Potential growth (snowball effect)

---

### Statement Upload & Categorization

Users can:

- Upload bank statements
- Upload credit card statements
- Automatically categorize transactions into:
  - Food
  - Rent
  - Subscriptions
  - Transportation
  - Entertainment
  - Other

---

### AI Spending Suggestions

The system will:

- Analyze spending habits
- Suggest where to spend less
- Recommend what to do with saved money
- Provide simple, clear financial guidance

---

### Growth Projection Graph

Display:

- Potential financial growth
- Long-term savings projections
- Visual investment growth

---

### Net Worth Calculator

Calculate:

- Total assets
- Total liabilities
- Net worth

---

### Budget System

Create a personalized budget that:

- Automatically monitors spending
- Tracks spending by category
- Keeps users on track toward financial goals

---

### Credit Score Tracking

Allow users to:

- Track their credit score
- Understand what affects their credit score

---

### Bank Account Integration (Future Idea)

Potential feature:

- Connect bank accounts
- Manage all money from one app

---

### Smart Budget Limits

AI will:

- Set spending limits per category
- Send notifications when limits are reached

---

### Subscription Manager

Users can:

- Track subscriptions
- Identify unused subscriptions
- Cancel unnecessary subscriptions

---

### Savings Goals

Users can:

- Add goals (wedding, trip, emergency fund)
- Move money into savings
- Track progress toward goals

---

### Financial Health Score

Generate a single number:

**0–100 Financial Health Score**

Based on:

- Spending-to-income ratio
- Savings rate
- Debt level
- Financial habits

This creates:

- Instant personalization
- Easy understanding
- Strong demo impact
